CB2: a Collaborative Natural Language Instruction Game

CB2 is a deployed re-implementation of the CerealBar collaborative natural language interaction platform.

Play With Other Humans → Play With a Baseline Bot →

You may have to wait a bit to play with the bot due to limited compute capacity. We recommend coordinating with a friend to play with another human, because low traffic periods are likely to entail very long waits for a partner.

python3 -m pip install cb2game

Collect Sets of Cards

Your goal is to collaborate with your partner to collects sets of cards. Each set consists of three unique cards that cannot have repeating color, shape, or count. Each set give you and your partner together one point!

Work Together!

You play as either the leader or the follower. Combine your unique advantages of you and your partner to improve your score. As a leader, you can see the entire board and give instructions to the follower. As a follower, you can get much more done per turn

Make the Most of Limited Time!

The game ends when you run out of turns, but for each completed set you get more turns. Each turn is limited, so work think and work fast. The turn will end when you run out of time, so keep an eye on the clock!

CB2 is an AI Research Platform

CB2 is a flexible research and data collection platform made to study natural language instructions grounded in a 3D environment.

User-tested UI

The user interface was developed iteratively by incorporating feedback from testers. All actions have keyboard shortcuts for ease of use.

Modular Art Assets

High quality, modular art assets custom designed to elicit diverse instructions.

Procedural Map Generation

Each interaction takes place in a unique, diverse world thanks to our map generation algorithm.

Built for Serving ML Agents

The platform makes it easy to run human-human, human-bot, or even bot-bot games using backend Python APIs, including an OpenAI gym API.

Custom Scenarios

Custom maps and scenarios can be crafted to support diverse research needs and controlled experiments.
